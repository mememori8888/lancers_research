  > Year Ended December 31, 2019
  > Operating income, as reported: $2,957
  > Operating income, excluding special items: $2,957
  >
  > The Company determined that, as of October 10, 2017, its Employee population, for purposes of determining the median Employee under the SEC's rules, consisted of approximately 59,387 individuals, whether employed on a full-time, part-time, or temporary basis.
  > There were no material changes to the Company's Employee population or compensation programs for 2019 that the Company believed would significantly impact the pay ratio disclosure for 2019, as compared with 2018.
