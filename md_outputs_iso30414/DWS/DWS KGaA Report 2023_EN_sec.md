### 従業員1人あたりEBIT / EBIT per employee
- **指標ID**: `ebit_per_fte`
- **値**: 3,476.45 t€/FTE
- **記載ページ**: 4, 12
- **根拠**:
  > **Income Statement (Page 4):**
  > 13. Net income: 541,357 t€.
  > 12. Income taxes: 168,666 t€.
  > 11. Interest and similar expenses: 29,182 t€.
  > 9. Other interest and similar income: 19,579 t€.
  >
  > **Other Information (Page 12):**
  > "The average number of staff employed in the financial year 2023 was 207 (previous year 173), of whom 186 (previous year 157) were non-tariff employees and 20 (previous year 16) were tariff employees."
- **計算式**:
  1.  税引前利益 (Profit before tax) = 純利益 (Net income) + 法人税等 (Income taxes)
      = 541,357 t€ + 168,666 t€ = 710,023 t€
  2.  EBIT = 税引前利益 (Profit before tax) + 純支払利息 (Net interest expense)
      純支払利息 = 支払利息 (Interest and similar expenses) - 受取利息 (Other interest and similar income)
      = 29,182 t€ - 19,579 t€ = 9,603 t€
      EBIT = 710,023 t€ + 9,603 t€ = 719,626 t€
  3.  従業員1人あたりEBIT = EBIT / 平均従業員数
      = 719,626 t€ / 207 FTE = 3,476.45 t€/FTE

### 人的資本RoI / Human capital ROI (HCROI)
- **指標ID**: `human_capital_roi`
- **値**: 16.53
- **記載ページ**: 4, 12
- **根拠**:
  > **Income Statement (Page 4):**
  > 13. Net income: 541,357 t€.
  > 12. Income taxes: 168,666 t€.
  > 11. Interest and similar expenses: 29,182 t€.
  > 9. Other interest and similar income: 19,579 t€.
  > 4. Staff expenses: 46,338 t€.
- **計算式**:
  ISO30414の一般的なHCROI計算式: (純利益 + 税金 + 利息費用 + 報酬費用) / 報酬費用
  1.  純支払利息 (Net interest expense) = 支払利息 (Interest and similar expenses) - 受取利息 (Other interest and similar income)
      = 29,182 t€ - 19,579 t€ = 9,603 t€
  2.  HCROI = (純利益 (Net income) + 法人税等 (Income taxes) + 純支払利息 (Net interest expense) + 人件費 (Staff expenses)) / 人件費 (Staff expenses)
      = (541,357 t€ + 168,666 t€ + 9,603 t€ + 46,338 t€) / 46,338 t€
      = 765,964 t€ / 46,338 t€ = 16.53
