### 人的資本RoI / Human capital ROI (HCROI)
- **指標ID**: `human_capital_roi`
- **値**: 2.484
- **記載ページ**: 5, 45
- **根拠**:
  > **Page 5 (Income statement for the period from January 1 to December 31, 2022):**
  > Interest income from: 22,141 € m.
  > Commission income: 6,095 € m.
  > Net trading result: 7,176 € m.
  > Wages and salaries: 4,687 € m.
  > Social security contributions and expenses for pensions and other employee benefits: 2,032 € m.
  > Other administrative expenses: 8,640 € m.
  > Other operating expenses: 8,484 € m.
  > Depreciation, amortization and write-downs of and value adjustments to tangible and intangible assets: 1,596 € m.
- **計算式**:
  Total Income = 22,141 + 6,095 + 7,176 = 35,412 million EUR
  Personnel Costs = Wages and salaries + Social security contributions and expenses for pensions and other employee benefits = 4,687 + 2,032 = 6,719 million EUR
  Operating Expenses excluding personnel costs = Other administrative expenses + Other operating expenses + Depreciation, amortization and write-downs = 8,640 + 8,484 + 1,596 = 18,720 million EUR
  HCROI = (Total Income - Operating Expenses excluding personnel costs) / Personnel Costs = (35,412 - 18,720) / 6,719 = 16,692 / 6,719 = 2.484
