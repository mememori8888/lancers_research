## 生産性・採用

### 従業員1人あたりEBIT / EBIT per employee
- **指標ID**: `ebit_per_fte`
- **値**: EUR 278,799 per employee
- **記載ページ**: 5, 47
- **根拠**:
  > **Page 5 (Income Statement):**
  > - "Net income (loss)": 1,919 million EUR
  > - "Income taxes": (485) million EUR
  > - "Interest expenses": 7,590 million EUR
  >
  > **Page 47 (Employees section):**
  > - "The average number of full-time equivalent staff employed during the reporting year was 35,848"
- **計算式**:
  *   税引前利益 (PBT) = 純利益 (Net income (loss)) + 法人税 (Income taxes)
      PBT = 1,919 million EUR + 485 million EUR = 2,404 million EUR
  *   EBIT = 税引前利益 (PBT) + 支払利息 (Interest expenses)
      EBIT = 2,404 million EUR + 7,590 million EUR = 9,994 million EUR
  *   従業員1人あたりEBIT = EBIT / 平均従業員数 (FTE)
      従業員1人あたりEBIT = 9,994,000,000 EUR / 35,848 = 278,799.48 EUR ≈ EUR 278,799 per employee

### 従業員一人当たり売上 / Revenue per employee
- **指標ID**: `sales_per_employee`
- **値**: EUR 802,003 per employee
- **記載ページ**: 5, 47
- **根拠**:
  > **Page 5 (Income Statement):**
  > - "Interest income from": 11,447 million EUR
  > - "Current income from": 1,089 million EUR
  > - "Income from profit-pooling, profit-transfer and partial profit-transfer agreements": 354 million EUR
  > - "Commission income": 9,050 million EUR
  > - "Net trading result": 6,811 million EUR
  >
  > **Page 47 (Employees section):**
  > - "The average number of full-time equivalent staff employed during the reporting year was 35,848"
- **計算式**:
  *   総営業収益 (銀行の売上相当) = 受取利息 (Interest income from) + 経常収益 (Current income from) + 損益計算書、利益移転、部分利益移転契約からの収益 (Income from profit-pooling, profit-transfer and partial profit-transfer agreements) + 手数料収益 (Commission income) + 純取引結果 (Net trading result)
      売上 = 11,447 million EUR + 1,089 million EUR + 354 million EUR + 9,050 million EUR + 6,811 million EUR = 28,751 million EUR
  *   従業員一人当たり売上 = 総営業収益 / 平均従業員数 (FTE)
      従業員一人当たり売上 = 28,751,000,000 EUR / 35,848 = 802,003.46 EUR ≈ EUR 802,003 per employee
